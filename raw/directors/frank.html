Frank Borzage (1894–1962)

Actor | Director | Producer
Frank Borzage was born on April 23, 1894 in Salt Lake City, Utah, USA. He was an actor and director, known for A Farewell to Arms (1932), 7th Heaven (1927) and Stage Door Canteen (1943). He was married to Juanita Scott, Edna Skelton and Rena Rogers. He died on June 19, 1962 in Hollywood, Los Angeles, California, USA. See full bio »
